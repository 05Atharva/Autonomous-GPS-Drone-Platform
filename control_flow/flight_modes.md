# Autonomous Flight Modes

## Auto Takeoff
- Drone arms after GPS lock
- Home position is saved at takeoff
- Ascends to predefined altitude automatically

## Waypoint Mission
- Mission created in Mission Planner
- Drone navigates through GPS waypoints autonomously
- Altitude maintained using GPS + barometer

## RTL (Return to Launch)
- Drone returns to saved home location
- Used as a safety and recovery mode
