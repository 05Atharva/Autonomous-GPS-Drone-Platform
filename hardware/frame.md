# Airframe Details

## Frame Used

- Model: S500 Quadcopter Frame
- Configuration: X-type quadrotor

## Reason for Selection

- Space for GPS, telemetry, and wiring
- Structural stability for autonomous flight
- Compatibility with medium-sized BLDC motors
- Modular and maintainable design

## Application Context

- GPS-based navigation
- Stable takeoff and landing
- Payload mounting for sensors and telemetry
